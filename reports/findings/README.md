# Finding Templates

Finding templates should help convert verified technical evidence into professional reports. A template is not a finding by itself; it becomes one only after manual verification on an authorized target.

## Suggested Template Fields

- Title
- Severity
- Affected asset
- Summary
- Business impact
- Technical impact
- Evidence
- Reproduction conditions
- Root cause
- Remediation
- Retest steps
- References
- CWE
- OWASP category
- CVSS vector and score
- Confidence

## Writing Rules

- Be precise about what was proven.
- Do not exaggerate scanner output.
- Include enough evidence to reproduce safely.
- Keep sensitive data out of the report unless the client explicitly requires it.
- Separate internal notes from client-ready text.
