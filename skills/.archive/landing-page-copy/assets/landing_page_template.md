## FAQ

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. Every workflow in the vault is no-code (n8n, Zapier, Make) or low-code (one config file). If you can edit a Google Doc, you can ship.

**How much time does this take per week?**
Two hours is enough to get value: one Profit Lab call (60 min) plus one workflow build session. Members who put in four ship faster, but we designed it for busy founders.

**Is this for beginners or advanced operators?**
Both. There's a 14-day onboarding track for people new to AI automation, and a separate "operators" channel for members running > $10k/mo where conversation gets technical.

**What's the ROI?**
One automated workflow that replaces a freelancer or saves you 10 hours/week pays for the membership for the next 12 months. That's the bar we hold ourselves to in the first 30 days.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. Monthly billing, cancel from your dashboard, no questions asked. Refund window is 30 days from join date.

> Notes for whoever ports this for a different offer:
> - The "30 minutes vs 6 hours" line works because it's specific. Vague time savings ("get hours back!") don't convert.
> - The guarantee is *outcome-based* ("ship one revenue workflow"), not just generic 30-day. Outcome guarantees convert harder but require you to mean it.
> - Three testimonials minimum, with names and numbers; logos work if testimonials are unavailable.
> - Don't add bonuses. The offer is the room, not a stack of PDFs.
